Shirt

A cloth garment for the upper body Originally an undergarment worn exclusively by men

Suit

A set of outer clothes made of the same fabric and designed to be worn together, typically consisting of a jacket and trousers or a jacket and skirt.

Blouse

A woman's upper garment resembling a shirt, typically with a collar, buttons, and sleeves

Skirt

A garment fastened around the waist and hanging down around the legs, worn by women and girls

Tie

Attach or fasten with string or similar cord

Trouser

A piece of clothing covering the lower part of the body from the waist to the foot and including separate sections for each leg

Shorts

Short trousers that reach only to the knees or thighs

Jacket

An outer garment extending either to the waist or the hips, typically having sleeves and a fastening down the front

Coat

An outer garment worn especially for warmth

Sweater

A knitted garment worn on the upper body, typically with long sleeves, put on over the head

GLove

A covering for the hand worn for protection against cold or dirt and typically having separate parts for each finger and the thumb

Vest

An undergarment worn on the upper part of the body, typically having no sleeves

Shoe

A covering for the foot, typically made of leather, having a sturdy sole and not reaching above the ankle

Pyjama

A loose-fitting jacket and trousers for sleeping in

Sock

A piece of clothing worn on the feet and often covering the ankle or some part of the calf

Boot

A sturdy item of footwear covering the foot and ankle, and sometimes also the lower leg

Hat

A shaped covering for the head worn for warmth, as a fashion item, or as part of a uniform

Slipper

A comfortable slip-on shoe that is worn indoors

Cardigan

A knitted woollen sweater that you can fasten at the front with buttons

Tracksuit

A loose, warm set of clothes consisting of a sweatshirt and trousers with an elasticated or drawstring waist, worn when exercising or as casual wear

Jeans

A hard-wearing casual trousers made of denim or other cotton fabric

Belt

A strip of leather or other material worn, typically round the waist, to support or hold in clothes or to carry weapons

Scarf

A length or square of fabric worn around the neck or head

Gown

A long elegant dress worn on formal occasions
