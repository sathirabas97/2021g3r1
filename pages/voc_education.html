School

An institution for educating children

Principal

A person who is in a leading position at school

Teacher

A person who teaches in school

Student

A person who is studying at school, university or any other places

Book

Written or printed work consisting of pages glued together

Classroom

A room in a school where lessons take place

Library

A place where a collection of books are kept to read or borrow

Study

Learn a particular subject or subjects

Time table

A list of the times when particular subjects or activities will happen

Homework

Work that a teacher gives to students to do at home

Grade

A particular level of rank

Syllabus

The subjects in a course of study

Attendance

The action of going to or being present at a place or event

Admission

The act of agreeing that something is true

Discipline

A practise of training to obey rules or a code of behaviour

Leave

Permission to be absent

Examination

A formal test that take to show the knowledge

Club

An association dedicated to a particular interest or activity

University

A high-level educational institution in which students study for degree

Lecturer

A person who teaches at a university or college

Staff

A group of people working at a same school or organization

Result

How something ended or outcome of an action

Degree

The qualification students obtain when they complete the university or college

Research

Careful consideration of study regarding a particular concern
