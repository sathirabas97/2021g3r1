Crusader

a fighter in the medieval Crusades

Empire

an extensive group of states or countries ruled over by a single monarch, an oligarchy, or a sovereign state

King

the male ruler of an independent state, especially one who inherits the position by right of birth

Queen

the female ruler of an independent state, especially one who inherits the position by right of birth.

Kingdom

a country, state, or territory ruled by a king or queen.

Bow

a knot tied with two loops and two loose ends, used especially for tying shoelaces and decorative ribbons.

Arrow

a weapon consisting of a thin, straight stick with a sharp point, designed to be shot from a bow.

Warrior

(especially in former times) a brave or experienced soldier or fighter

Medieval

relating to the Middle Ages.

Pirate

a person who attacks and robs ships at sea.

Slavery

the state of being a slave.

Sultan

a Muslim sovereign.

Era

a long and distinct period of history.

Govern

conduct the policy, actions, and affairs of (a state, organization, or people) with authority.

Century

a period of one hundred years

Ancient

belonging to the very distant past and no longer in existence
