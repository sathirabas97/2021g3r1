Benefit

advantage of a product or service, usually derived from its features

Commercial

a paid advertisement on radio or TV

Feature

special characteristic of a product, usually leading to certain benefits

Chairman

person who heads a board of directors; head of a company

Claim

an application for payment under an insurance policy

Manager

person responsible for day-to-day running of a business or company department

Reception

the place where visitors and clients report on arrival at a company

Shareholder

person who owns shares or holds stock in a company or corporation

Agreement

an arrangement made between people, companies, countries etc; contract

Retire

to leave employment, esp. because of old age

Party

the person or persons forming one side of an agreement

Warrant

to give formal assurance; to guarantee

Bonus

additional pay given to an employee as an incentive or reward

Employer

person or firm who employs people

Interview

a formal meeting in which a person applying for a job, a course, a visa etc is questioned

Resign

to give up a job
