Vehicle

A conveyance that transports people or objects

Passenger

A traveler riding in a vehicle but not operating it

Transportation

The movement of humans and goods from one location to another

Airport

A place where airplanes take off and land that has all the services

Drive

Operate or control a vehicle

Traffic

Vehicles or pedestrians traveling in a particular locality

Bus

A vehicle carrying many passengers

Pedestrian

A person who travels by foot

Car

A motor vehicle with four wheels

Ride

Sit and travel on the back of animal

Journey

The act of traveling from one place to another

Bicycle

A two-wheeled vehicle that you sit on and move by turning the two pedals

Lorry

A large truck designed to carry heavy loads

Road

Hard surface built for vehicles to travel along

Ship

A vessel that carries passengers or goods

Aeroplane

A vehicle designed for air travel that has wings and one or more engines

Train

A set of several carriages that are connected to each other

Pilot

A person who operates the flying controls of an aircraft

Captain

The person in command of a ship

Crowd

Large group of people who have gathered together

Arrive

The act of leaving

Departure

Reach a destination

Ticket

A piece of paper or card that gives the holder a certain right,specially to travel

Luggage

Cases used to carry belongings when traveling
