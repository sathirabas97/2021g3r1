Doctor

A person who is qualified to treat people who are ill

Teacher

A person that passes knowledge to students, usually at school

Engineer

A person who develops solutions to technical problems

Nurse

A person trained to help a doctor look after the sick or injured

Policeman

A male member of a police force

Dentist

A person that can fix problems you have with your teeth

Chef

A person that prepared food for others, often in a restaurant

Postman

A person who is employed to deliver or collect letters and parcels

Waiter

A person who serves customers at their tables in a restaurant

Electrician

A person that works with electric circuits

Photographer

A person who takes photos

Scientist

An expert in science, especially one of the physical or natural sciences

Soldier

A person who works for the army

Secretary

A person employed in an office who types letters and keeps records

Accountant

A person that works with the money and accounts of a company

Tailor

A person who makes clothes for others

Driver

A person who drives vehicle

Carpenter

A person that makes things from wood including houses and furniture

Author

A person who writes books or novels

Architect

A person who designs buildings

Fisherman

A person that catches fish

Farmer

Someone who owns or runs a farm

Journalist

A person that makes new reports in writing or through television

Lawyer

A person that defends people in court and gives legal advice
