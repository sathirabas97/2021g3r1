Bank

Financial institution where customers can deposit or withdraw money and and make loans

Hospital

An institution in which sick or injured persons are given medical or surgical treatment

Court

A place where trials and other legal cases happen

Hotel

A commercial establishment offering lodging to travelers

Market

Place where buyers and sellers can gather to exchange goods and services

Prison

A building where criminals are kept as a punishment

Mall

A large retail complex containing a variety of stores and often restaurants and other business establishments

Museum

A building in which objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and exhibited

Bakery

A place where bread and cakes are made or sold

Church

Place for public Christian worship

Temple

A building reserved for spiritual rituals and activities such as prayer and sacrifice

Mosque

A Muslim place of worship

Pharmacy

A shop or hospital dispensary where medicinal drugs are prepared or sold

Gym

A place where you can go to exercise using machines

Stadium

An athletic or sports ground with tiers of seats for spectators

Park

A large public garden or area of land used for recreation

Supermarket

A large self-service shop selling foods and household goods

Office

A room, set of rooms, or building used as a place for commercial, professional, or bureaucratic work

Shop

A building or part of a building where goods or services are sold

Zoo

An establishment which maintains a collection of wild animals

Restaurant

A place where people pay to sit and eat meals that are cooked and served on the premises

Cinema

A theatre where films are shown for public entertainment

Factory

A building or buildings where people use machines to produce goods

Playground

An outdoor area provided for children to play in, especially at a school or public park
