Football

Hiking

The activity of going for long walks, especially across country

Hockey

Game between two teams of players who each have a curved stick

Jogging

The activity of running at a slow, regular speed

Chess

The action or practice of shooting with a gun

Tennis

A game in which two or four players strike a ball with rackets over a net stretched across a court

Yoga

Type of exercise in which you move your body into various positions

Weightlifting

Activity of lifting barbells or other heavy weights

Karate

A Japanese art of self-defense employing hand strikes and kicks to disable or subdue an opponent

Team

A group of players forming one side in a competitive game

A contest in which people or teams compete against each other in a particular sport

Score

A record of points made or lost

Tournament

A series of contests between a number of competitors, competing for an overall prize

Gymnastics

Exercises developing or displaying physical agility and coordination
